How to play

You got 10 minutes, guess as many words as you can!

1. Click "Begin Game" to start!

2. Start guessing by pressing any letter on keyboard!

3. Every correct letter is 10 points!

4. More Correct guesses in a row, higher the combo!

5. When 10 minutes are over, submit your score!

5. Press spacebar to pause whenever you want!
